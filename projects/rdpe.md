# RDPE

A visual editor for particle systems.

**50,000+ lines of Rust + WGPU + Egui**

## What It Is

Real-time preview, node-based workflow, and enough parameters to make your GPU nervous.

Build particle effects visually, tweak in real-time, export for use in games or other projects.
